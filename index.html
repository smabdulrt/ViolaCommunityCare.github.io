Welcome to Viola Community Care

Viola Community Care is an NDIS provider working with people with disability to make it easier for them to access services they need under the NDIS support service. We provide professional care and support services to the participants of the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). We have trained and qualified staff having expertise in their respective fields. Our utmost priority is to provide the necessary support and help people with disabilities so that they can enjoy their life to the fullest without being worried about finances and their circumstances.

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) gives people with a disability greater choice over the supports they receive. The NDIS funds services that enable people to achieve their goals, increase independence, develop skills for everyday life and participate in the community.

What we do?

We provide a variety of NDIS supports to suit a wide range of participants. All our services are personalised and provided by qualified disability support workers and personal care attendants. We follow the NDIS Price Guide for all services. We can help you achieve the goals outlined in your NDIS plan. This may include short-term goals like increasing daily living skills, or long-term goals like developing the physical ability to participate in community events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide services to people living with disability in order to improve the quality of their lives. We continue to fulfil our mission by identifying and developing services that are needed, to enable people who find themselves facing barriers or exclusion to be included in our communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide quality and timely services to NDIS participants, enabling them to enhance the quality of their lives. We are dedicated to ensuring accessibility to a range of services that promote a healthy and prosperous lifestyle.

We provide the following services:

Group/Centre Activities


We help people living with a disability in improving their morality, independence, and mobility through our NDIS social activities. Through our various programs rendered by trained professionals, we cater to the mental and physical well-being of our participants. Viola Community Care is led by experts from the field that approach and attend our participants with the utmost care and empathy, meanwhile ensuring that they are helped in all possible ways.

Community Participation Activities


At Viola Community Care, we understand the importance of participants enjoying the recreational aspect of life. We go beyond that to understand that they need to live just like any other person and lay emphasis on helping them go about their social interactions such as visiting the library or watching a play, or partaking in hobby classes through our NDIS community participation activities.

Assist Travel/Transport


We help assist our participants with their transport and travel requirements. If you want to travel to and from your place of work, study, or community participation, we can help you find a support driver. We ensure that the support drivers that we source for you are well trained and are properly briefed about your needs and requirements.

Household Tasks


We provide support with household tasks to empower the participant to become capable of handling their daily chores on their own or with minimal assistance. Over time, participants are trained in varied household tasks, which give them more independence and mobility.

Development Life Skills


Development of daily living and life skills focuses on training and development activities undertaken by the participant or their support workers to increase their ability to live as independently as possible. These may include individual or group activities like skills in daily life activities, communication and social skills, problem solving and managing funding of supports.

Assist Personal Activities


We are able to provide you NDIS assistance with your personal care. We can assist with a range of everyday activities in the comfort of your home including mobility and hoist transfers, showering or bathing, dressing and grooming etc.

Assistance with daily life tasks – Group/Shared living


Our support revolves around helping NDIS participants thrive in a shared living environment, whether the arrangement is temporary or long term. Participants will be assisted and/or supervised as they complete day to day activities at home, helping them to live their lives as independently and autonomously as possible.

Support Coordination


This includes short-term and long-term support focuses on living at home, participating in your community, and coordinating your support. Services include: getting in touch with the support, to coordinate services, concerning housing and tenancy obligations, relating to mentoring, peer support, and development of skill sets etc.

Early Childhood Support


We support families to better understand their child’s support needs, identifying the most appropriate response to address those needs. Its about identifying the types of supports that provide for a child to develop their potential. The main goals is to enable children with disability or developmental delays to develop their skills and take part in day-to-day activities. By providing supports as early as possible in a child’s life, ECEI aims improve the outcomes of young children as they grow and learn.
